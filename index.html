How to Play

  • Guess the secret word one letter at a time.
  • Each wrong guess adds a part to the hangman.
  • You lose if the hangman is fully drawn!

Power-Ups & Hints

  • Show Hint: Click the button to reveal a clue about the word.
  • Reveal Letter: Instantly shows one correct letter on the board.
  • Remove Mistake: Erases your last wrong guess.
  • 50/50: Removes half of the incorrect letters from the keyboard.

Rule: If you choose **5 or fewer** chances, all power-ups are available. For more chances, only the first two are active.
